# Short Video Scripter

A Claude AI skill that writes scroll-stopping short video scripts for Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts — under 60 seconds.

Built by [@beinginvested](https://instagram.com/beinginvested)

---

## What it does

Drop a topic. Get back a ready-to-record script. No fluff, no labels, no explanations — just the spoken words.

Every script follows an invisible 5-beat structure:

**Shock Hook → Pain → Value → USP → Close**

The viewer never feels these as separate sections. It flows like one confident person talking on camera.

---

## What's inside

- **6 hook pattern families** — Direct Attack, Negative Revelation, Missed Opportunity, Ego Poke, Blunt Statement, Contradiction
- **Strict voice rules** — short sentences, spoken English, no buzzwords, no LinkedIn tone
- **Banned word filter** — 25+ words and phrases that get auto-rejected (no "game changer," no "deep dive," no "unlock")
- **Built-in quality gate** — every script passes 6 internal checks before output
- **4 calibration examples** — AI tools, geopolitics, developer tools, finance — so the tone never drifts

---

## How to use it

### In a Claude Project

1. Go to your Claude Project → Project Knowledge
2. Upload `SKILL.md`
3. Start a new conversation and give it a topic
4. You'll get back only the script — nothing else

### As custom instructions

Copy the contents of `SKILL.md` and paste into your Claude project's custom instructions or system prompt.

## Example

**Input:** "Make a video about why keeping money in a savings account is losing you money"

**Output:**

> Your savings account is making you poorer every month.
>
> You check your balance. It's growing. Two percent interest, maybe three. Feels safe. Feels smart.
>
> But inflation is running at five to six percent. That means every rupee sitting in your savings account is losing three percent of its buying power every year. You're not saving. You're slowly bleeding.
>
> A simple index fund has averaged twelve percent annually over the last twenty years in India. You don't need to pick stocks. You don't need to time the market. You just need to move your money from a place that loses value to a place that builds it.
>
> The safest thing you can do with money is not let it sit still.
